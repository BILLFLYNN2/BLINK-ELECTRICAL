What we do

Four ways we look after your home

Electrical contracting, solar and battery installation, and home automation — one licensed team, for homeowners across Sydney's Northern Beaches.

01 — Residential Electrical

General electrical work, done to a high standard

Switchboard upgrades, rewiring, lighting and power — the everyday work that keeps a home safe and running well, carried out by a qualified electrician who treats your house like his own.

  • Switchboard & safety switch upgrades
  • Rewiring, fault finding & repairs
  • Renovations, extensions & new builds
  • EV charger installation
02 — Solar

Solar systems designed around your roof

Every home on the Beaches catches the sun differently. We size and design each system around your roof, your orientation and your household — then install it to Clean Energy Council standard.

  • Free site assessment & system design
  • Panels & inverters built for coastal conditions
  • Government rebate guidance
  • Monitoring app set up on handover
03 — Batteries

Store the power your solar makes

A battery lets you use your own solar power after the sun goes down, and keeps the essentials running if the grid drops out — increasingly common during Sydney storm season.

  • Battery sizing for your household
  • Retrofits onto existing solar systems
  • Backup circuits for essential loads
  • Premium brands, properly installed
04 — Automation

A smart home that stays out of the way

Lighting, climate and security you can run from your phone — wired in properly at switchboard level, not stuck on with a battery-powered gadget.

  • Smart lighting & switch upgrades
  • App & voice control set up
  • Security & camera integration
  • Works alongside your solar & battery

Good to know

Frequently asked questions

Do you service the whole Northern Beaches?

Yes. We're based in Manly and work right along the peninsula, from Manly and Freshwater through to Mona Vale, Newport, Avalon and Palm Beach.

Are you licensed for solar and battery installations?

Yes. Blink Electrical is a fully licensed NSW electrical contractor, accredited to design and install solar and battery storage systems to Clean Energy Council standards.

How quickly can you come out for a quote?

We aim to get back to every enquiry within one business day, and can usually offer a site visit within the week.

Do you provide fixed, upfront pricing?

Yes. Every job is quoted in writing before we start, so there are no surprises on the invoice.

Do you work on older homes as well as new builds?

Most of our work is on the character homes the Northern Beaches is known for — Federation cottages, 1970s beach houses and everything since — as well as new builds and renovations.
